Trainables
==========

.. contents:: :local:

Trainables cover two branches, :any:`Processes <BaseProcess>` and :any:`Models <DN3BaseModel>`. They both could consist
of potentially learnable parameters, but largely, :any:`Processes <BaseProcess>` are the way in which `Models` are
trained.
